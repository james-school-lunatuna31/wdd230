Welcome

The Fairfax County Chamber of Commerce is a vibrant and dynamic organization located in the heart of Fairfax County, Virginia. Our mission is to foster community development opportunities and provide valuable information about local economics. As a hub for local businesses and community members, we host a variety of events throughout the year, providing opportunities for networking, learning, and collaboration. Whether you're interested in connecting with city officials, learning about the top 10 public projects currently in development, or simply want to stay informed about what's happening in our community, the Fairfax County Chamber of Commerce is your go-to resource
